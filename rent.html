Urban Innovations: Renting and Leasing

Renting and Leasing

Urban Innovations has a variety of properties available for renting and leasing in the City of Calgary including apartments, condos, homes, and retail space. Most properties in this pool are within a 10 minute commute to the city core.

RESIDENTIALPROPERTIES

Properties are most commonly leased on a one year term with an automatic month to month renewal following the initial term. Standard Residential Tenancy forms are used for all residential agreements in conformity with the Residential Tenancy Act of Alberta.

Leased residential properties are all substantially renovated in desirable developed neighbourhoods of the NW and SW quadrants of the City of Calgary.

As of 2024 the Urban Innovations rental portfolio includes select prime condos in Calgary’s downtown core.

If you are interested in the availability of any rentals, contact Urban Innovations for a list of current inventory.

COMMERCIALPROPERTIES

Retail space is commonly leased on a 5-year term with first options for renewal. These properties are storefront style usually not larger than 1000 sq. feet per bay and are located in high profile traffic areas of the city with good visibility and accessibility.

Commercial tenants may negotiate leasehold improvements that specifically address the unique needs of their business. If no properties are available when desired, we will be pleased to include you on a candidate list so that when a property becomes available, you will be notified.
