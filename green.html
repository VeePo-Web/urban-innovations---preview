Urban Innovations thinks green!

At Urban Innovations we understand our responsibility to our community, both local and global, to manage our resources and reduce our geological footprint. We value environmental stewardship through common-sense decision making, responsible project planning, and conscious efforts to increasingly mitigate our environmental impacts in our redevelopment processes.
We think green.

All multifamily buildings are retro fitted with newer energy efficient windows and high efficiency boilers. All replacement windows are upgraded to Low E with Argon for additional R value.

Investment locations strategically target the inner city where higher density land use minimizes travel for work, shopping and leisure. Properties are located near LRT stations or popular transit routes.

Redevelopment is focused in the inner city where current zoning reduces urban sprawl land requirements for single family and condo development growth.
