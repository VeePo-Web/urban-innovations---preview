Urban Innovations: Real Estate Development

Randy Steinwand

president

In acquiring a first four-plex property in 1987, Randy Steinwand began his journey in purchasing, renovating and leasing properties in Alberta and British Columbia. Since that time, Randy has managed the restoration of dozens of choice residential properties focusing on the prime redevelopment market within a 10-minute commute to downtown Calgary.

Real Estate Redevelopment

Renovation Projects

With a focus on the Calgary market, Urban Innovations specializes in low density single and multi-family (R-CG to M-C2 zoned) properties and selective commercial projects near the downtown core. Urban Innovations focuses on revitalizing strategically located under-performing properties. Upon completion, some properties are leased by a holding company, others are offered for resale through Urban Innovations or the public Multiple Listing Service (MLS) system.

Construction Projects

Redevelopment projects include new construction on prime inner city locations. Urban Innovations offers attached residential duplex homes featuring open plan designs, upscale features and double garages in choice established neighbourhoods. New homes are for sale through Urban Innovations or the MLS public system and come complete with the Alberta New Home Warranty Program, National Home Warranty Program, or a tailored warranty program offered by Urban Innovations.
